What Is a User Story?

A user story is a short, simple descriptions of a feature told from the end-user's perspective, focusing on who needs it, what they need, and why they need it. User story exists to ensure development stays focused on user value rather than just technical specifications.

Standard format:

As a [user], I want to [do something], so that [benefit].

User stories:

i. Focus on value, not implementation

ii. Encourage collaboration

iii. Support incremental delivery

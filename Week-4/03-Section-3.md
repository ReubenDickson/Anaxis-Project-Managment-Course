CBT User Personas (Your Context)

At minimum, your CBT system has:

1. Student
    - Takes exams
    - Views results
    - Needs clarity, speed, and reliability
2. Instructor/Examiner
    - Creates exams
    - Manages questions
    - Reviews results
3. Administrator
    - Manages users
    - Oversees system usage
    - Handles configuration

You may identify additional personas if justified.

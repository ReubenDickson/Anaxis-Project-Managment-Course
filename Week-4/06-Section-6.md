Scope Boundaries (Critical Skill)

Scope boundaries define the precise LIMITS of a project, explicitly outlining what work is included and, crucially, what is excluded (project exclusions). These boundaries prevent scope creep, manage stakeholder expectations, and keep teams focused by defining deliverables, geographic reach, and constraints. Scope boudaries are documented in the scope statement.

Every requirement must pass one test:

Is this required for the CBT frontend MVP?

If not, it is out of scope.

Scope discipline protects:

- Timelines
- Teams
- Delivery credibility

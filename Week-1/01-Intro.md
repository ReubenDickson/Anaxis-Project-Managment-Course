Introduction to Software Project Management
Week Objective (Read This First)

This week sets your mental foundation for the entire course.

Before you touch Agile, Scrum, GitHub Projects, or sprints, you must clearly understand:

- What a software project actually is

- Why software projects fail so often

- What project management means in tech, not textbooks

- Your responsibility in this course as a project manager in training


By the end of this week, you should think differently about software projects.

What You Will Achieve by the End of Week 1

By completing this week, you will:

- Understand how software projects differ from traditional projects

- Understand the role of a Project Manager in IT/software teams

- Understand why tools and process matter more than job titles

- Understand the CBT capstone project at a high level

- Produce your first written project artifact

No coding is required this week.

WEEK 2: Project Initiation & Project Charter

Objective: This week, you will formally initiate the CBT software project.


In the real world, projects fail very early - not because of bad code, but because:

- Goals are unclear

- Scope is undefined

- Stakeholders are not identified

- Assumptions are left undocumented


Your job this week is to prevent those failures before they happen.

By the end of this week, your project must have:

i. A clearly stated purpose

ii. Defined boundaries

iii. Identified stakeholders

iv. Written assumptions and constraints

v. A professional documentation structure in GitHub


What You Will Achieve by the End of Week 2

By completing this week, you will:

i. Understand how software projects are formally initiated

ii. Write a real Project Charter

iii. Define scope at a high level (what is in/what is out)

iv. Identify stakeholders and their interests

v. Create a professional documentation structure


These are non-negotiable project foundations.
